===================================================================================
Other calculations
===================================================================================

There are other calculations in VolFe and new ones can be added!

Here we show examples for a few that might be useful:

- :doc:`Example 4a <Examples/4a. other_fug_coeff>`: Calculate fugacity coefficients at various *P* and *T*.

- :doc:`Example 4b <Examples/4b. sol_const>`: Calculate solubility constants at various *P*, *T*, and melt compositions.

- :doc:`Example 4c <Examples/4c. other pure_sol>`: Calculate pure solubilities for H\ :sub:`2`/O and CO\ :sub:`2`.

- :doc:`Example 4d <Examples/4d. other_isobar>`: Calculate isobars for H\ :sub:`2`/O-CO\ :sub:`2`.

- :doc:`Example 4e <Examples/4e. other_error>`: Monte Carlo approach for analytical uncertainties on melt composition.
