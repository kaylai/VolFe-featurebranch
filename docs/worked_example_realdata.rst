===================================================================================
Applications
===================================================================================

These are examples of how you can use VolFe to explore natural data or model different systems.

- :doc:`Example 5a <Examples/5a marianas>`: Example for a melt inclusion and submarine pillow glass dataset from the Marianas arc (Brounce et al., 2014; Kelley and Cottrell, 2012; Cottrell et al., 2021) from `Hughes et al. (2025) <https://doi.org/10.30909/vol/imvc1781>`_. Includes calculations of *P* \ :sup:`v` \ :sub:`sat`, *f* \ :sub:`O2` from melt sulfur content, closed- and open-system re- and degassing paths, isobars, and Monte Carlo error propagation of analytical uncertainties.
